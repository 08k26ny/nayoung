About

With a background in psychology, industrial engineering, and HCI, I study how people interact with emerging technologies. My work combines qualitative and quantitative UX methods to understand user behavior, cognition, and experience.

I am especially interested in human robot interaction, AI service design, teleoperation, and interfaces that support older adults and other diverse users.
